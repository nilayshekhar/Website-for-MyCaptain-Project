About Starater

Starater should be your most trusted recommendation resource for quality entertainment. As an aggregator of movie, we want to provide you with a comprehensive guide to what's Fresh and what's and new in theaters and at home. And the Starater is just the beginning. We also serve you with the opportunity to rate the movies. We know that no other website allows you to rate any movie in their website. And we also feel that no other website should have the right to decide which movie you should like and which movie you should not. That is the sole reason why we created this website because we wanted to give you the opportunity to rate what you like. Thanks for taking the time to understand what we are and what we stand for, and we look forward to your valuable contributions to our site and social platforms.
